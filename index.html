GoSki Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026 · Last Updated: May 14, 2026

Summary. GoSki is a privacy-first ski tracking app. All your tracks, runs, and analytics are stored locally on your iPhone. GoSki does not collect, sell, or share personal data. The only outbound network request is to a public weather API, and it does not include any personal identifier.

1. Introduction

GoSki ("we", "the app") is developed by Xiao Guo. This Privacy Policy explains how GoSki handles information when you use our iOS application. We designed GoSki so that your activity on the mountain stays yours — we do not operate any servers that store your data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any account information. GoSki does not require an account.
  • We do not collect or transmit your location data, GPS tracks, or activity history to any server we control.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Facebook SDK, no Mixpanel, no Umeng, etc.).
  • We do not display advertisements and do not integrate any ad SDK.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IDFA, or IDFV for tracking purposes.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored only on your device, inside the app's private sandbox. It never leaves your iPhone unless you explicitly export it yourself.

4. Permissions We Request

Location (When In Use & Always)

GoSki uses your precise location solely to record your ski runs. The "Always" authorization is requested so tracking can continue when the screen is off or you switch to another app during a run. Location data is processed on-device only and used to compute track statistics; it is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

5. Third-Party Services

GoSki makes a single category of outbound network request:

  • Open-Meteo Weather API (open-meteo.com) — used to fetch public weather forecasts for ski resorts. Requests contain only a resort's approximate coordinates, no user identifier. Open-Meteo is a free, non-commercial public weather service.

GoSki does not integrate any other third-party service, SDK, or tracker.

6. Data Security

Because your data stays on your iPhone, its security benefits from the iOS sandbox, device encryption, and your passcode / Face ID / Touch ID. We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using a strong device passcode.

7. Data Deletion

To delete all data collected by GoSki, simply uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes the app's sandbox and therefore all tracks, runs, and preferences. You may also clear individual tracks from within the app.

8. Children's Privacy

GoSki is a general audience fitness and navigation app. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us information through the app, please contact us to confirm; however, because we do not operate user accounts, there is typically no data to remove beyond uninstalling the app from the child's device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are material, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, include a notice in the app.
