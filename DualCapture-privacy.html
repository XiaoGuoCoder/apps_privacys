Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Introduction

DualCapture ("we", "our", or "the App") is a camera recording application developed by Xiao Guo. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

DualCapture is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • Camera Access: The App requires camera access solely to provide its recording functionality. Video is captured and processed entirely on your device.
  • Photo Library Access: The App requests permission to save recorded videos to your photo library and to display a recent media thumbnail. We do not access, upload, or analyze your photos or videos.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store infrastructure. We do not collect or store any payment or financial information.

Data Storage

All data generated by DualCapture — including recorded videos, usage preferences, and purchase status — is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

DualCapture does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external communication is with Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchase verification.

Children's Privacy

DualCapture does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
