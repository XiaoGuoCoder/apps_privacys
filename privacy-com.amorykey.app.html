Amorykey Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Amorykey ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the limited data the App processes. By using Amorykey, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Introduction

Amorykey is an AI-assisted messaging companion. It helps you craft chat replies and expressive messages, offers an in-app companion chat experience, and provides a custom keyboard extension for generating replies directly inside other apps. The App does not require account registration and does not maintain user profiles on our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, sell, or share the following:

  • We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, phone number, or email address.
  • We do not collect your precise or approximate location.
  • We do not access your contacts.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or track you across other apps and websites.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device (using a shared App Group container between the main app and the keyboard extension) and is not uploaded to us:

  • Your persona settings, relationship tags, intimacy level, and "time known" preferences.
  • Your companion chat history, affinity progress, and related in-app state.
  • Your membership/subscription status and remaining free-usage counts.
  • App settings and preferences.

This data remains on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

4. Data Sent to Third-Party AI Services

Important: To generate replies, polish messages, and understand screenshots, the content you provide is transmitted to a third-party AI provider for processing. We do not use this content for any purpose other than returning a result to you.

When you actively request AI generation, the following may be sent to our AI provider:

  • Text you enter or provide as context (for example, the conversation snippet you want a reply to, or the message you want polished).
  • Images you choose (for example, a chat screenshot you select) so the AI can read and respond based on their content.
  • Your in-app persona and relationship settings, used only to tailor the generated wording.

AI Provider: Alibaba Cloud DashScope (the Qwen model service). This data is transmitted over an encrypted connection and processed to return generated text to you. We do not send this data to any other party. Please refer to Alibaba Cloud's privacy terms for how they process data on their platform.

Character illustration images used inside the companion feature are loaded from our content delivery domain and do not involve uploading your personal data.

5. Keyboard Extension & Full Access

Amorykey includes a custom keyboard extension. To generate replies, the keyboard needs "Full Access" (network permission).

  • The keyboard only sends text to the AI provider when you explicitly tap a generate/polish action. It does not silently log or transmit your general typing.
  • The keyboard does not include keystroke logging, analytics, or ad tracking.
  • If you do not enable Full Access, network-based generation in the keyboard will not function.

6. In-App Purchases

Amorykey offers auto-renewable subscriptions. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Subscription status is verified via Apple's StoreKit and stored locally on your device.

7. Photos

When you attach a screenshot or image, the App uses the system photo picker. We access only the specific images you choose; we do not scan or read your photo library. Selected images are used solely for the AI to understand and respond to their content, as described in Section 4.

8. Data Security

Data transmitted to the AI provider is sent over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. Locally stored data is protected by the device's standard iOS data protection. Because we do not operate user accounts or central servers holding your personal content, the attack surface for your data is minimized.

9. Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data:

  • You can clear companion chat history and in-app data from the App's settings/debug tools.
  • Deleting the App removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • To manage or cancel a subscription, use your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

10. Children's Privacy

Amorykey is not directed to children under the age of 17 and contains mature, romance-oriented conversational content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
