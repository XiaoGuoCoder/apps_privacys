Math Flashcards Kids 完全离线运行，不会收集以下任何信息：

App 仅在您的设备本地存储以下数据，从不离开您的设备：

3. 第三方服务

本 App 不集成任何第三方 SDK，包括但不限于：广告 SDK、分析 SDK、推送 SDK、社交登录 SDK。

4. 网络访问

5. 儿童隐私 (COPPA / GDPR-K)

本 App 主要面向 4-12 岁儿童。由于我们不收集任何信息，因此完全符合 COPPA（美国儿童在线隐私保护法）、GDPR-K（欧盟儿童一般数据保护条例）以及 Apple App Store「儿童类目」相关要求。

6. 应用内购买与广告

本 App 无应用内购买、无广告、无外部链接跳转。

7. 数据安全

由于本 App 不联网、不收集数据，因此不存在数据泄露的可能性。本地数据由 iOS 系统沙盒机制保护。

8. 隐私政策变更

Math Flashcards Kids Privacy Policy

100% OfflineNo Data CollectionKids First

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

This app is a fully offline math practice app designed for children. We solemnly promise: this app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data. No network requests. No third-party SDKs. No ads.

1. We Collect No Information

Math Flashcards Kids runs entirely offline and never collects:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, UUID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics, taps, or crash logs
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or any privacy-sensitive permissions

2. Local Data Storage

The app only stores the following on your device locally and never leaves your device:

  • Level progress and stars
  • Collected cards
  • Parental settings (difficulty, sound)

Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently.

3. Third-Party Services

This app integrates no third-party SDKs: no ads, analytics, push, or social login SDKs.

4. Network Access

This app makes no network requests and works fully in airplane mode.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

This app primarily targets children aged 4-12. Since we collect no information, the app fully complies with COPPA, GDPR-K, and Apple App Store Kids Category requirements.

6. In-App Purchases & Ads

This app contains no in-app purchases, no ads, and no external links.

7. Data Security

Since the app neither connects to the network nor collects data, no data breach is possible. Local data is protected by the iOS sandbox.
