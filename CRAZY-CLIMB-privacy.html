Privacy Policy

CRAZY CLIMB - Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CRAZY CLIMB handles user information. The App is fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, account)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, MAC address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, calendars, or photo library metadata
  • Health, financial, or browsing data
  • Analytics, crash reports, or usage telemetry

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following locally on your device only:

  • Game progress (unlocked levels, completed levels, achievement state)
  • Free-Climb best height, total wins, total falls
  • User-selected profile avatar (an image you pick from your photo library)

This data uses Apple UserDefaults and the App Documents directory. Deleting the App removes all of it.

4. Photo Library Access

If you tap the avatar on the Profile tab, the App presents Apple PHPickerViewController, which runs out-of-process within iOS itself. The App never gains access to your photo library and does not require any permission. Only the single image you pick is delivered to the App and saved locally as your avatar.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no advertising networks, and no remote services.

6. Data Security

All data resides on your device, protected by iOS sandboxing and your device passcode/biometric protection. Because nothing is transmitted, there is no server-side risk.

7. Data Deletion

Uninstall the App from your device and all stored progress, statistics, and the avatar image will be permanently removed. There is no remote copy.

8. Children's Privacy

CRAZY CLIMB does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. As the App collects no information at all, it is safe for users of any age.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at the same URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us
