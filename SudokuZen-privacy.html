Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

Sudoku Zen ("the App") is developed by Xiao Guo. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Summary: Sudoku Zen does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely offline and stores all data exclusively on your device.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Sudoku Zen does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other sensitive data

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Game Progress: Current puzzle state, selected difficulty, elapsed time
  • Statistics: Games played, win rates, best times, streaks per difficulty level
  • Preferences: Theme settings, hint limit toggle, sound/haptic preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Sudoku Zen does not integrate any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • Analytics platforms (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • Advertising networks (no ads of any kind)
  • Crash reporting tools (no Crashlytics, no Sentry)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Cloud storage services

4. Network Usage

The App makes zero network requests. It is fully functional in airplane mode and does not require an internet connection at any point.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and no data is transmitted over any network, there is no risk of data interception or server breach. Your game data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

6. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by Sudoku Zen, simply uninstall (delete) the App from your device. All locally stored preferences and game progress will be permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Zen does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages. No age verification is required because no personal information is ever collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
